TACTICAL K9 FIELD MEDIC

[1] K9 CPR - CARDIOPULMONARY RESUSCITATION
1. CHECK: No pulse, no breathing. Lay dog on RIGHT side.
2. COMPRESSIONS: Place hands over widest part of chest. Compress 1/3 to 1/2 depth.
3. RATE: 100-120 beats per minute (Stayin' Alive tempo).
4. BREATHS: 2 breaths for every 30 compressions (Close mouth, blow into nose).
[2] SNAKE BITE (VENOMOUS)
1. IDENTIFY: Do NOT attempt to catch snake. Note color/head shape.
2. RESTRICT: Keep K9 calm and immobilized. Movement spreads venom.
3. POSITION: Keep the bite area BELOW the heart.
4. NEVER: Do not cut the wound or try to suck out venom. No ice.
5. ACTION: Immediate transport to vet for anti-venom.
[3] HEAT STROKE
1. SYMPTOMS: Excessive panting, dark red gums, collapse.
2. COOL: Move to shade. Use cool water on belly/paws. DO NOT use ice water.
[4] NARCAN DEPLOYMENT
1. SIGNS: Respiratory depression, pinpoint pupils.
2. DOSE: 4mg Intranasal. Repeat every 3 minutes until K9 is alert.
