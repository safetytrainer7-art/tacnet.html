VIRGINIA TACTICAL LEGAL REFERENCE

LEO USE ONLY - AMHERST COUNTY S.O.

VA CODE § 18.2-250.1 (POSSESSION): Guidance on marijuana detection and how recent legislative changes affect "Probable Cause" for vehicle searches.
VA v. HURD (2010): Virginia-specific ruling on the reliability of a K9's "alert" and the documentation required for a valid search.
US v. PLACE (FEDERAL): The core ruling that a dog sniff is not a search under the 4th Amendment.
RODRIGUEZ v. US (FEDERAL): The "Stop Clock" ruling. Warning: Do not extend the duration of the stop just for the K9 arrival.
VA CODE § 3.2-6500 (ANIMAL CARE): Legal requirements for K9 welfare and protection while in service.
ILLINOIS v. CABALLES: Confirms sniff is lawful if the stop is otherwise valid.
