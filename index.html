TACTICAL LEGAL LIBRARY

FLORIDA v. HARRIS (2013): K9 alert reliability. Focus on training records and certifications, not just field track record.
RODRIGUEZ v. US (2015): Officers may not extend a traffic stop to conduct a dog sniff without reasonable suspicion.
ILLINOIS v. CABALLES (2005): A dog sniff during a lawful traffic stop does not violate the 4th Amendment.
US v. PLACE (1983): A canine sniff of luggage in a public place is not a "search" requiring a warrant.
FLORIDA v. JARDINES (2013): Using a drug dog on a homeowner's porch (curtilage) requires a warrant.
